🌱 Import from Tiller

Upload your Tiller transaction export so we can learn from the categories you already assigned and turn repeated patterns into future-ready rules.

How this works
  • Column D categories are treated as your ground-truth labels
  • Repeated labeled merchants are turned into reusable rules for future imports and syncs
  • Imported transactions are also stored as historical examples for the AI classifier
  • Transactions with c - prefix → Coaching Business (Schedule C)
  • Transactions with m - or w - prefix → Whitford House / Rental Activity
  • All other categorized transactions → Family / Personal
  • Transfers, payments, and uncategorized rows are skipped
